Fried Plantains

Description

Fried plantains - This is a sweet-savory dish that features thick slices of ripe plantains lightly salted and deep-fried in oil till golden brown.
The result is a caramel-like brown crust that is savory-sweet and a soft and very sweet interior.

Ingredients

  • 3 plantains ripe
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups Oil

Intructions

  1. Cut the ends of the plantains off and off the skin. slice on the bias into about ¼ to ½ inch thickness. Season with salt and mix together.
  2. Heat the Oil in a medium pan over medium to high heat.
  3. Carefully add the plantain inside the oil in a single layer (don't overcrowd the Oil).
  4. Fry the first side for about 3 to 4 minutes. Then flip to the other side and fry for another 3 to 5 minutes or till both sides are golden brown.
  5. Remove the plantains from the oil and transfer them to a colander.
  6. Repeat with the remaining plantains if you have more and Serve hot!
