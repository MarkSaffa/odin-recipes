Rice and Beans

Description

Nigerian rice and beans recipe is a Nigerian staple food that is simple, delicious, and very easy to cook.

  • 1 cup beans
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 small Onion diced
  • 6 to 7 cups water
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or coconut oil

Intructions

  1. Pick your beans to get rid of any dirt
  2. Rinse the beans and transfer it to a pot, Add the diced onion and Water, cover it up, and leave it to cook for about 40 to 50 minutes or till you can easily crush it with your finger.
  3. Rinse the rice thoroughly until the water comes out clear stir in the rice, butter/oil, and salt with the beans. Add water if necessary.
  4. Return to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, cover and cook for 20 minutes without removing the lid.
  5. Fold the rice and beans together gently and serve with your desired sauce, soup, or stew. Enjoy!
